Frequently Asked Questions

How fast is the delivery?

Most orders are delivered instantly (within 0–5 minutes). During peak hours it may take up to 15 minutes.

Is this safe and legit?

Yes. We use official game recharge channels via authorized API partners. Your account is completely safe — we never ask for your password.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayNow, GrabPay, credit/debit cards, and bank transfer. More payment options coming soon.

What if I enter the wrong User ID?

We validate your User ID before processing payment. You'll see your in-game name displayed for confirmation. If something goes wrong, contact us on Telegram immediately.

Can I get a refund?

Since diamonds are delivered instantly to your game account, refunds are generally not possible. However, if there's an error on our side, contact us on Telegram and we'll resolve it.

Do you support Indonesian MLBB accounts?

Not at the moment. Indonesian accounts use a separate payment system. We currently support Singapore, Malaysia, Philippines, and Brazil regions.
