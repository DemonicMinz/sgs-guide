About Singapore Gaming Syndicate

Singapore's biggest verified Mobile Legends community — built for players who take the grind seriously.

What is SGS?

SGS is a members-only Telegram community for Singapore-based MLBB players. It's where scrim teams are formed, rank partners are found, and strategies get shared. We were founded by local Mythic-rank players who were tired of solo-queueing into feeders, and built it into the largest verified Singapore MLBB community.

Why Join?

  • Every member is verified — no smurf accounts, no time-wasters.
  • Daily ranked lobbies from Epic all the way to Mythic Glory.
  • Live tier list and hero guides updated from real match data every 6 hours.
  • Regular local tournaments with cash and in-game prizes.
  • Direct Q&A with Mythic-rank players in the community chat.
  • Coaching sessions where high-rank players help climbers improve.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify my MLBB account with SGS?▾

After joining the SGS Telegram group, follow the pinned verification message. The process takes under a minute — you screenshot your in-game profile and send it to the verification bot. Verified-only policy keeps scrims smurf-free.

Is SGS only for Mythic-rank players?▾

No. SGS has daily lobbies from Epic through Mythic Glory — any serious Singapore player is welcome. We also run coaching sessions where higher-rank members help climbers improve.

Does SGS cost anything to join?▾

No. SGS is completely free. We run on community goodwill, occasional tournament sponsorships, and the belief that local scenes grow faster when they stay open.

Is SGS affiliated with Moonton?▾

No. SGS is an independent community-run group. Hero data on this site comes from the OpenMLBB community API. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Moonton.
